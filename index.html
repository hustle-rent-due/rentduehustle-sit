Order Process

How to Place an Order

Ordering from Hustle Rent Media is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to get started:

Step 1: Consultation

Contact us through our booking form to discuss your project requirements. We'll schedule a free consultation to understand your needs and provide a quote.

Step 2: Project Details

Provide us with all necessary information about your project, including:

  • Your brand guidelines (if available)
  • Specific design requirements
  • Deadlines and timeline expectations
  • Examples of styles you like

Step 3: Payment & Agreement

Once we agree on the scope and pricing, we'll send you an invoice. A 50% deposit is required to begin work on most projects.

Step 4: Creation & Revisions

We'll create your design and provide updates throughout the process. You'll have opportunities for revisions to ensure complete satisfaction.

Step 5: Final Delivery

After final approval and remaining payment, we'll deliver your completed files in all required formats.

Payment Methods

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
